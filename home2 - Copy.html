Example of attacks:

Michael Calce(Mafiaboy)a 15-year-old student living in Canada, used his computer to shut down the websites of some of the world’s biggest companies, including CNN, Dell, Amazon, Yahoo and eBay. He was one of the most wanted cyber criminals in the world. He called it Project Rivolta and its impact in 2000 shocked the US stockmarket. It’s estimated it caused $1.7 billion dollars in financial damage across the 16 companies that were hacked.
